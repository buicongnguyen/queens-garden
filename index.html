Queen's Garden

Place one queen in every row, column, and color patch. Queens may not touch diagonally, even at one-cell distance.

A calm logic puzzle dressed like a royal conservatory.

Quick rules

  • Exactly one queen per row
  • Exactly one queen per column
  • Exactly one queen per colored region
  • No two queens may touch diagonally

How to play

  • On mobile, tap once for a dot mark, tap again for a queen, and tap once more to clear.
  • On desktop, click with the active tool, or right-click or Shift+click for a quick mark.
  • Every colored region must hold exactly one queen.
  • Rows and columns also need exactly one queen each.
  • Queens may not sit on touching diagonals.

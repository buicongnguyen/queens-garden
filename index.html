Mobile play

The board now stays front and center, while this drawer holds the longer notes.

  • Tap once for a dot mark, tap the same cell again for a queen, and tap once more to clear.
  • Swipe across cells to lay down several dot marks at once.
  • Open this menu for board size, fixed levels, best times, reset, reveal, and hint.

How to play

  • Every colored region must hold exactly one queen.
  • Rows and columns also need exactly one queen each.
  • Queens may not sit on touching diagonals.
  • On desktop, click with the active tool, or hold the right mouse button and hover to paint marks.
